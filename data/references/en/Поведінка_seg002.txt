Everything can change. Today I had a productive meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Norway. Norway is one of our greatest helpers in defense and the leader of the PEARL program, which enables us to purchase American weapons, primarily air defense systems. Norway has significantly assisted us with air defense. We spoke again about precisely these matters — protecting the skies, our joint steps, and our joint plans both with Norway and with other partners in Europe. There is also a new support package from Norway — 400 million for energy and for Ukraine's resilience, with a total of 8 billion in Norwegian assistance over the year. This is a very tangible contribution. Thank you. I also continued personnel changes today, both in the regions and in the Security Service of Ukraine. There will be further decisions. Just now I also spoke with Prime Minister Yuliia Svyrydenko about recovery efforts and work across all regions. I thank everyone who is literally working for the sake of life. We agreed with the Prime Minister to develop a special program to support specifically those people who work directly on repair crews, around the clock, in the most difficult circumstances. The government will propose a way to substantially increase the wages of such specialists for every month of winter. And please, be sure to pay attention to air raid alerts. There is intelligence information that the Russians are preparing a new massive strike. Drones to exhaust our air defenses and missiles. They want to exploit the cold. This strike could come within the next few days. Please take care of yourselves. Take care of Ukraine. And help those who are struggling most and who need support. Together we will overcome everything. Glory to Ukraine!
