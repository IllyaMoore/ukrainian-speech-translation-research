In Kharkiv, the Russians struck directly at heating, at energy for people, at ordinary life — ballistic missiles against energy infrastructure. In Kherson, energy repair crews were working to restore electricity supply after Russian strikes. All of this only attests to the fact that Russia does not genuinely, seriously engage with the diplomatic efforts that civilized countries are trying to conduct with it.
