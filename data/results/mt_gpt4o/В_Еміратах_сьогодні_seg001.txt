Today, Ukrainian, American, and Russian delegations are there. There was already an important conversation, as there have not been such trilateral meeting formats for a long time. They are discussing the parameters for ending the war. They should now have at least some responses from Russia. The main thing is for Russia to be ready to end this war, which it started itself. Ukrainian positions are clear. I have defined the framework for dialogue for our delegation, and the delegation is constantly in contact. Rustem Umerov, Kyrylo Budanov, Davyd Arakhamia, Serhiy Kyslytsya. It is necessary not only for Ukraine to want to end this war and achieve full security, but also for a similar desire to somehow emerge in Russia. It is good that there is more interaction within the air forces and analysis of circumstances. Conclusions are very much needed to make the system more effective. I also instructed our team to work out our agreements with our partners on missiles for air defense as quickly and as thoroughly as possible. Now we need a positive signal here in Ukraine in the actual supply for our sky defenders. Logistics need to be accelerated as much as possible. I also held a special energy meeting.
