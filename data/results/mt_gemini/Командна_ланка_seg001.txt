Fellow Ukrainians, a brief update on the day. Regarding energy, restoration is virtually ongoing after the strikes. This applies not only to Kyiv, but also to Kharkiv and the region, Zaporizhzhia, Dnipropetrovsk region, Dnipro, Kryvyi Rih, Chernihiv region, Sumy region, all of them. All regions where the situation is most difficult. Undoubtedly, Kyiv faces the greatest challenges right now, and I expect the Kyiv authorities to intensify their work. Many cities and communities are ready to help. The government is ready to help. All instructions, opportunities, everything is available. 174 brigades are working. This is a significant resource that has now been directed to Kyiv. There are some results, but not enough. A significant number of houses are without heating, and some of them have been without heating for a long time. I have instructed the government to allocate additional resources. The Ministry of Internal Affairs is working along its lines. We are scaling up support and heating points. Everyone in the government will work with partners to obtain additional equipment as quickly as possible. I thank all our friends. And these are various European countries. From Norway to Italy, Poland, Germany, the Baltic States. Many others are currently supporting Ukraine, our cities, and doing a lot for Kyiv. Separately, I spoke today with the Minister of Defense about protecting energy facilities. Also, with the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, the command echelon of the Air Force must be significantly faster in providing protection. Today there was also a full report from our delegation. The guys returned after trilateral meetings. For a long time, there was again a format with the Americans and the Russians. They managed to discuss various issues of a more military nature. Regarding steps to end the war and real control and monitoring, there are issues that need to be prepared for the next meeting. We previously discussed that the teams would meet again on Sunday. It would be good if this meeting could be accelerated. Ukraine will be as prepared as possible on all matters to be discussed before agreement. Ukraine always is and will be on the side of peace, and Russia is the only one through whom this war is still ongoing. Real results of diplomacy are needed so that there is no impression that the Russians are using both the negotiation process and, with a very cynical and harsh purpose, are pushing back new measures of pressure on Russia that could work. And pressure is needed, precisely pressure, its consequences, the result of sanctions, the result of blocking Russian operations. All this works to stop wars. Partners should not forget about this. I thank everyone who is with Ukraine. Thank you to everyone who helps us. Glory to Ukraine!
