I want to note the work of the Ministry of Internal Affairs. I thank everyone involved. Everything is being done as clearly and specifically as possible regarding generator and equipment reserves, regarding real assistance to cities, especially Kyiv, regarding heating points and support for people. Unfortunately, many buildings in the capital still remain without heat. Maximum brigades, maximum equipment are being deployed to help people. I spoke with Prime Minister Yuliia Sviridenko about programs that can support both the economy and communities. As for generators and business operations, we are offering maximum favorable programs to help small businesses purchase and maintain generators. Government officials must present all of this to society today. There are also solutions to directly support each person and each person in repair brigades. This involves additional payments for each month of this emergency situation. We agreed on this today with the Prime Minister, also with the Minister of Energy. What format the payments will take and how people will receive these payments. Every worker engaged in emergency recovery work will receive an additional payment of 20 thousand hryvnias for January, February, and March of this year. Twenty thousand for each month. Details from the government will follow additionally. And of course, I thank each country, all the leaders who are providing necessary assistance. There are already and will be more energy packages. The European Union, Italy in particular, Germany, Lithuania, Britain, Poland, Japan, America. I thank all of you who are with us, who are with Ukraine. Glory to Ukraine!
