I wish health to every Ukrainian. Today there are many tasks, including for our diplomatic team. Ukrainian representatives are reporting to me almost hourly. They are currently located in the Emirates. Today there are Ukrainian, American, and Russian delegations there. An important conversation has already taken place, because such tripartite meeting formats have not occurred in a long time. They are discussing the parameters for ending the war. Right now they should have at least some responses from Russia. The main thing is that Russia should be ready to end this war, which it started itself. Ukrainian positions are clear. I have defined the framework for dialogue for our delegation, and the delegation remains in constant contact. Rustem Umerov, Kyrylo Budanov, Davyd Arakhamia, Serhiy Kyslytsya. Tomorrow General Hnatyev, the Chief of the General Staff, and HUR representative Skibytskyy will also join the negotiations. Regarding the substance of today's talks, it is still too early to draw conclusions. We will see how the conversation goes tomorrow and what results there will be. It is necessary that not only Ukraine desires to end this war and achieve complete security, but that Russia somehow develops a similar desire as well. Today I was briefed by Ukraine's Defense Minister Fedorov. We discussed the situation on the front and our technological responses. I thank every unit that is defending our Ukrainian positions and noticeably increasing the technological component. The level of Russian losses in December is 35,000. This is very serious. I also spoke with the minister about personnel matters, not only in the ministry but also in the branches of the armed forces, particularly in the Air Force. It is good that there is more interaction within the Air Force and analysis of complex situations. Conclusions are very much needed so that the system becomes more effective. I also instructed our team to work as quickly as possible and in maximum detail on our agreements with our partners regarding air defense missiles. Yesterday there was a positive signal in my conversation with President Trump regarding Patriot PAC-3 missiles. Now we need a positive signal here in Ukraine in actual supplies for our sky defenders. Logistics need to be accelerated as much as possible. I also conducted a special energy briefing. Every day begins in exactly this format—repair brigades, emergency services, energy companies, the State Emergency Service of Ukraine. Everyone is working around the clock, there are results in restoration, but overall the situation remains very difficult. This is especially true for Kyiv and Dnipropetrovsk region, Dnipro, Kryvyi Rih, Kharkiv and Zaporizhzhia region, Sumy region, and Chernihiv region. We separately reviewed the situation in Odesa.
